My Future Goals

My future goals are to firstly design a game with either JavaScript or Unity, and my other goal is to have a successful career in the future.

Firstly, I want to develop a game because I have always been engrossed in games as a child and was always curious how these game developers made these functions of the games happen. I always had an obsession with shooter games like Call of Duty and Halo when I was younger and wanted to know how the game developers got the bullet trajectory perfectly. Then the final step towards my curiosity was when I discovered Fortnite in 2017 and from then on I wanted to design a game that complex and with that much functionality. All that said, I am going to start small starting off.

I want to be able to have a successful career in the future because without that I wouldn't have a stable life. Some career paths I am exploring are become a lawyer or engineer. I think I would do well in both of these industries because I have very good communication skills and a passion of debate which is needed for a lawyer. Then, I also have a big passion of technology and love designing things online and making videos, this is why I am leaning more towards the engineering sector.
